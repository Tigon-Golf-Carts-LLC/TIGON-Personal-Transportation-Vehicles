### Problem-Solution Pairs

**Problem:** Cart losing power on hills
**Solution:** Check battery charge level and connections. Verify motor controller settings. Consider upgrading to higher voltage system or performance package for consistent hill climbing power.

**Problem:** Steering feels loose or unresponsive  
**Solution:** Inspect steering components for wear. Check tire pressure and alignment. Lubricate steering linkage and adjust as needed. Replace worn bushings or tie rod ends.

**Problem:** Cart not charging properly
**Solution:** Test charger output voltage and amperage. Inspect charging port and connections for corrosion. Check individual battery cell voltages. Clean terminals and verify proper charger operation.

**Problem:** Excessive tire wear
**Solution:** Check wheel alignment and tire pressure. Inspect suspension components for wear. Evaluate driving habits and terrain conditions. Rotate tires regularly and maintain proper inflation.

### Industry Terminology Definitions

**Lithium Battery:** Advanced battery technology offering longer life, faster charging, and consistent power output compared to traditional lead-acid batteries.

**Regenerative Braking:** Energy recovery system that converts braking force back into battery power, extending range and reducing brake wear.

**Street Legal Package:** Complete conversion kit including lighting, signals, mirrors, windshield, and safety equipment required for road use in Pennsylvania.

**AC Motor:** Alternating current motor system providing smooth acceleration, variable speed control, and reduced maintenance compared to DC motors.

**Battery Management System (BMS):** Electronic system monitoring and protecting battery cells, ensuring optimal performance and safety.
